Educational Modules

Designed for first-generation immigrant women in the USA to learn about health, wellness, and navigating the healthcare system.

Preventive Care

Learn how to access preventive healthcare services and screenings.

Maternal Health

Support for pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum care.

Nutrition

Build healthy eating habits using affordable and culturally familiar foods.

Chronic Disease Management

Learn how to manage and prevent diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease.

Navigating U.S. Healthcare

Understand how to access healthcare services and your patient rights.
